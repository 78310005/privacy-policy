最后更新：2025-04-01

我们致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策解释了我们如何收集、使用和保护您在使用我们的应用程序时的信息。

我们收集的信息

我们不收集任何个人信息。所有身份验证数据都存储在您的设备本地，绝不会传输到我们的服务器或任何第三方。

由于我们不收集您的信息，我们不会将其用于任何目的。您的身份验证令牌仅保留在您的设备上。

我们实施适当的安全措施来保护应用程序内存储的数据。您的身份验证数据经过加密并仅存储在您的本地设备上。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-04-01

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

We do not collect any personal information. All authentication data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Since we do not collect your information, we do not use it for any purpose. Your authentication tokens remain only on your device.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect data stored within the application. Your authentication data is encrypted and stored only on your local device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us
